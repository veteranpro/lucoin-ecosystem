How LuCoin started

I didn't build this in a startup accelerator. I built it because my daughters kept forgetting what they earned. So I invented a currency. Named it after their baby brother, Lukas. Gave them a ledger book. Told them: you earn LuCoins, you write it down, you subtract when you spend.

Eight years old. Ten years old. Keeping books like accountants at Five Below and Target.

Then I taught them spreadsheets. Then I connected it to real crypto prices. LuCoin Ecosystem is that ledger book — digitized.

— Kam, founder & dad
